Fluffy French Toast Recipe

Description

This French toast recipe is different because it uses flour. I have given it to some friends and they all liked it better than the French toast they usually make!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl
  2. Slowly whisk in the milk
  3. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat
  5. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated
  6. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot
